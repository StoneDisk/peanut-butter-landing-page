Creamy Peanut Butter

Introducing the yummiest, creamiest peanut butter you've ever tasted! Perfect for snacking, our peanut butter is a hit with children and adults alike. Made with only the highest quality ingredients, it's a snack the whole family can enjoy. Spread it on toast, add it to smoothies or eat it straight out of the jar, however you choose to enjoy it, you won't be disappointed. Try it today and taste the difference!

Ingredients And Allergens

Roasted peanuts, sugar, emulsifier (monoglyceride), hydrogenated vegetable oil (cottonseed, soybean and rapeseed oil) to prevent separation, Vitamin B1, Vitamin E Acetate, Vitamin A Palmitate, Vitamin D3, iodized salt.

Allergen Warning:

**This product contains peanuts**
